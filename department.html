Education Department

This department will essentially design and implement WEF’s education programs, the very cornerstone of the Foundation. It will consist of the following personnel and/or their respective offices:

  • Principle's office

    The Principal’s Office will be in charge of ensuring the smooth running of the branch, by designing and implementing suitable strategies. The Office will also be responsible for the following tasks:
    Maintain a strong communication process with the central system
    Design and implement strategies for the branch
    Prepare and follow an annual budget
    Deal with any arising issues within the branch
    Conduct annual reviews and all necessary internal audit processes

  • Secretary

    The Faculty & Staff will include teachers from all disciplines, and all support system personnel (such as cleaners, gate guards, etc.)
  • Faculty & Stuff

    The Secretary will be in charge of assisting the Principal to further the goals and objectives of the branch literacy center.
  • Research Associates

    Research Associates will include any person involved in higher studies and/or related research work that may further the goals and objects of WEF’s Literacy Model.
  • Admission's Office

    This Office will primarily be responsible for admitting new students in the branch literacy center. And therefore it will be in charge of the followings tasks:
    Conduct outreach programs in order to infiltrate into marginalized communities
    Prepare enrollment processes for new students at the beginning of each year
    Assess and accommodate needs of the new students
    Correspond all necessary details with parents of WEF students

  • Children's Welfare Center

    This Center will be in charge of dealing with issues that concern a specific student or a group of students (mental issues, physical abuse, misbehavior etc.)

Human Resources Department

The Human Resources Department will be responsible for recruitment, volunteer management and all other administrative tasks of the branch. It will consist of the following personnel:

  • Employment Specialist

    who will be responsible for the following tasks:
    Evaluating employee needs for each department
    Designing and conducting recruitment processes
    Ensuring training and workshop for employees
    Talent Acquisition
  • Volunteer Management Officer,

    who will be in charge of recruiting, training and managing all volunteers.
  • Administrative Assistant,

    who will assist the department officers in furthering their goals and objectives.

Finance Department

This department will be responsible for designing and implementing strategies for generating adequate funding for the branch literacy center. The department will consist of the following personnel:

  • Finance Management Officer

    who will be responsible for designing strategies for ensuring funds for the next fiscal year.
  • Corporate Relations Officer

    who will be responsible for sponsor acquisition and maintaining a relationship with the corporate world.
  • Fundraising Specialist

    who will be responsible for conducting small fundraising events within the community.
  • Accountant

    who will be responsible for ensuring regular book-keeping (according to standard accounting practices).

Communications Department

This department will be in charge of the branch’s IT needs, graphics designing and inbound marketing and all other communications-related support system. It will consist of the following personnel:

  • Communications Officer

    who will be in charge of designing the groundwork for all communications projects.
  • Internal Communications Officer

    who will be in charge of all communications needs within the branch.
  • IT Team

    which will include work on graphics designing, social media updating, and all other necessary IT support systems for the branch.

Marketing and Public Relations Department

This department will be in charge of designing and conducting the branch’s marketing campaigns, and/or providing all relevant information if outsourced to another marketing company. The department will consist of the following personnel:

  • Marketing Specialist

    who will be in charge of all marketing campaigns
  • Public Relations Officer

    who will be in charge of maintaining the branch’s relationship with all external parties.
